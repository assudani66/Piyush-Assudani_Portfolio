3 Commercial Games That Feature Product Placement.

There are many ways to promote product using gaming apps. This article will focus on 3 different methods that have been proven by the following brands.

PUBG x TESLA

In a recent update to PUBG Mobile, Tesla has been added as a location in the virtual island of Erangel. The fictional Tesla factory is located on the northern coast and will provide players with vehicles and upgrades to their fleet.

FORTNITE X Michael Jordan

the partnership with the brand of the star Michael Jordan brought cosmetics to the game. Like skins, sprays, skateboards and tennis, along with challenges and a limited game mode. The so-called Downtown Drop was to fall into a New York-inspired city, perform giant jumps and collect coins to win the game.

Burnout paradise

Remember Obamas winning speech ,In its eternal quest to capture the youth vote, Barack Obama's presidential campaign has taken to the streets of Burnout Paradise, filling the game with billboards promoting the presidential hopeful

A great way to promote your business is by integrating your products into popular games. By doing so, you can increase brand awareness and boost sales. As digital marketing continues to grow, this technique will become more common. By making smart decisions about which games to partner with, you can reach a wide audience while saving money at the same time.
